Learn Web3 with
Arbitrum Layer 2

Web3 is transforming the internet by giving users ownership of their digital assets and data instead of relying on centralized platforms. At the heart of Web3 is blockchain technology, which makes transactions transparent, secure, and immutable.

Ethereum introduced smart contracts and decentralized applications, but as its popularity increased, the network became slower and transaction fees (gas fees) became expensive. This created the need for Layer 2 scaling solutions.

Arbitrum is one of the most popular Layer 2 networks built on Ethereum. It processes transactions off the Ethereum mainnet while still inheriting Ethereum's strong security. This allows users to enjoy faster transactions and significantly lower gas fees without compromising decentralization.

Why Ethereum Needed Layer 2

Ethereum is one of the most secure and widely used blockchains, but every transaction must be processed on the main network. When millions of users interact with decentralized applications at the same time, the network becomes congested. As a result, transaction confirmation takes longer and gas fees increase. Layer 2 solutions were introduced to solve these scalability challenges while keeping Ethereum secure.

What is Arbitrum?

Arbitrum is a Layer 2 scaling solution that works alongside Ethereum. Instead of processing every transaction directly on the Ethereum blockchain, Arbitrum executes transactions on its own network and then securely records the final result on Ethereum. This approach increases transaction speed, reduces costs, and maintains Ethereum's security and reliability.

Real-World Benefit

Scenario: Imagine purchasing an NFT or swapping tokens on Ethereum during peak hours. On Ethereum Mainnet, the transaction fee could sometimes be higher than the value of the transaction itself. By using Arbitrum, the same transaction is completed much faster and at a much lower cost while still benefiting from Ethereum's security. This makes Web3 applications more practical and accessible for everyone.
