Understanding Blockchain Components

🔑

Hash

A hash is a unique digital fingerprint generated from block data. Even a tiny change in the data creates a completely different hash output.

🔢

Nonce

A nonce is a number that miners continuously change until they discover a valid hash that satisfies the specific Proof of Work condition.

🔗

Previous Hash

Each block stores the hash of the previous block, creating a secure chain that connects every ledger transaction immutable structure together.

🛡️

Proof of Work

Proof of Work is a consensus mechanism where miners repeatedly calculate hashes until they find one that meets the required difficulty level constraints.
